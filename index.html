Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to cater to the diverse needs of our clients:

Personal Care

Our caregivers provide essential assistance with activities such as bathing, toileting, exercise, and medication reminders, ensuring our clients' well-being and comfort.

Household Care

Our caregivers provide essential assistance with activities such as bathing, toileting, exercise, and medication reminders, ensuring our clients' well-being and comfort.

Everyday Tasks

We make everyday tasks more manageable. Our caregivers accompany clients to medical appointments, assist with grocery shopping, and pick up prescriptions, ensuring they have the support they need.

Companionship

We believe in the power of human connection. Our companionship services encompass daily phone calls, regular check-ins, and engaging activities such as crafts and puzzles to promote social interaction and emotional well-being.

About Bartlett In-home Care

Established in 2020, Bartlett In-Home Care was founded with a mission to simplify the lives of families and individuals, focusing on delivering the utmost quality in-home assistance. Our dedicated team at Bartlett In-Home Care strives to deliver personalized home services with a commitment to convenience and transparency. We recognize the challenges of managing the demands of daily life, which is why we are dedicated to offering affordability and ease throughout the entire process.

About Christa Bartlett

Christa Bartlett is the founder and director of Bartlett In-Home Care. She holds a degree in Social Services & Gerontology from the University of Waterloo and a diploma in Activation Coordination in Gerontology from George Brown College.

With over 22 years of experience in the field, including roles as a Program Director and Events Coordinator, Christas' personal and professional experiences have provided her with a deep understanding of the needs and challenges faced by those seeking compassionate care and support.

Motivated by her dedication to enhancing the lives of seniors and their loved ones, Christa established Bartlett In-Home Care with a mission to create a nurturing and supportive environment for all the families Bartlett In-Home Care serves.
